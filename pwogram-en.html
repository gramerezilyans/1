Our programs

Each of our programs is connected to a series of issues present in the current education system.

Support for all students

We want to make a series of courses available to all students of all grades to help them better understand if they encounter difficulties in their regular schools. We also want to provide tools for parents to help their primary school children who cannot use the website.

Catch-up courses for 9th grade and NS4

These courses are special courses for all students taking the state exams. We want to help as many children as possible succeed.

Online school

To address the financial problems of many parents and the large number of schools destroyed recently, we want to set up a proper online school.

Educational media

This program was created with the aim of motivating students and giving them a taste for knowledge. We want to have a more serious ideological influence on the youth.

Other various courses for youth

In addition to the regular school courses, there are other skills that will greatly help young people.

Profesional courses

We want to provide young people with training so that they can access job opportunities after finishing school.

Philosophical courses

It is a free program open to everyone because we believe that philosophical and ideological training is very important in society.
