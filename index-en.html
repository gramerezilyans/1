What is Gramè Rezilyans?

Our mission

The mission of Gramè Rezilyans is always to identify the real problems related to education in Haiti and to seek solutions. We are a grassroots organization, we do not rely on the intervention of the State or other institutions to take action.

Our vision

Our vision is to have a meaningful impact on education in Haiti and to help many students and young people who are facing numerous challenges in their learning.

Our values

We have an ideological orientation based on dialectical materialist philosophy. Therefore, we believe in emancipation, in the power of children and young people to bring about meaningful change in the system. We also believe in each individual and in their potential to surpass themselves and to become more than what they are today.

What is Gramè Rezilyans?

Gramè Rezilyans is an initiative set up by a group of young people to address all the problems in the current Haitian educational system. We are a 100% online platform aiming to tackle issues where children lose many school days, programs are never truly followed, and most students fail the state exams… We want to help children, who are the future of the country.

Why Gramè Rezilyans ?

The mission of Gramè Rezilyans is to deliver a message of hope despite all the real problems in society. We do not aim to change these problems because we cannot. Our goal is to invest in children, in the quality of awareness they have of themselves and the society they live in.

What are the projects and programs of Gramè Rezilyans?

The most important programs and projects of Gramè Rezilyans include offering online catch-up courses for 9th grade and NS4 students preparing for state exams, a series of support courses for all grades, providing 100% online certified training, creating a 100% educational media platform, organizing numerous educational activities to genuinely motivate young people to learn, and offering a series of free courses on dialectical materialism, which is the ideological orientation of this platform. The programs will always be either free or low-cost.
